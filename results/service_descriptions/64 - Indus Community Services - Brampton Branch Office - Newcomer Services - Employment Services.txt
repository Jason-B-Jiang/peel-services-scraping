Facilitates the employment for new immigrants to Canada by familiarizing them with effective job-search practices and technique Employment services include: skills assessment resume and cover letter writing coaching for a successful interview labour market research, job fairs and networking opportunities access to a resource centre and use of computers, telephone, fax and photocopier Job Search Workshops: Designed to support newcomers with one-on-one employment counselling sessions and a variety of employment related workshops and job fairs. It is open to all newcomers. We also coordinate job fairs to facilitate the connection between employers and newcomers. These workshops take place bi-monthly and are free of charge
