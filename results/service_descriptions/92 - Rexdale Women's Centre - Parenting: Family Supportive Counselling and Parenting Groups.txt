Support for Women and Families provide three different programs: Parenting and Family Supportive Counselling: for newcomers, immigrant and refugee parents and grandparents who need support to raise healthy children Nobody's Perfect Parenting Groups: connects, shares and builds skills with peers serve newcomer parents and grandparents who lack the social networks and supports offers parenting groups effective discipline methods, information on Canadian institutions and service Family Drop-In: designed for children 2 to 12 years of age and their parents. The program provides children with the opportunity to develop skills in language, cognitive development, sensory development, creativity and gross motor skills development. Parents are involved in workshops and seminars that develop better parenting skills NOTE:All services are currently online. Contact us for updates or appointments
