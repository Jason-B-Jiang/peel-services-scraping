The program provides a suite of programs and services aimed at promoting the general wellbeing of members of the black community in Peel. The objectives of the Community Wellness and Development Program are to: Promote the health and wellness of members of the African/Black community through identified programs and services Enhance access to community resources and information Promote mental health awareness in the black community and improve access to mental health resources Reduce the incidence of domestic violence and abuse in the black/African Community Promote engagement and participation in community development activities Enhance labor market participation opportunities for members of the black community Practical support and encouragement
