The RSTP is a program designed to support the Sponsorship Agreement Holders (SAHs) of Canada, their Constituent Groups, Groups of Five and Community Sponsors interested in sponsoring refugees. The program provides: resources and services to meet ongoing information and training needs of private sponsors including Sponsorship Agreement Holders, their Constituent Groups, Groups of Five and Community Sponsors. This includes information about the refugee sponsorship process, how to complete government forms, the international refugee situation and other information needed to sponsor, welcome and settle refugees information is offered through webinars, e-training, workshops and conferences as well as through video and print materials and responses to individual queries Contact 416-290-1700 / Toll-free: 1-877-290-1701 for details
