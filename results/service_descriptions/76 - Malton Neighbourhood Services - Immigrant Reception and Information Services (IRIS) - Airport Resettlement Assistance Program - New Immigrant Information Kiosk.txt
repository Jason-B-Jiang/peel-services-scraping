Airport Resettlement Assistance Program - multi-lingual staff at Toronto Pearson Airport provide a warm welcome and assistance to government assisted and privately sponsored refugees * assist with ground transportation and connecting flights Services include: interpretation/translation in more than 34 languages facilitating the passage through Custom and Immigration at the airport arranging ground transportation and overnight accommodations providing winter clothing to government assisted refugees (Oct-April) flight rebooking and help solving other flight difficulties help solving baggage difficulties help to clients with special needs crisis intervention/assisting with medical emergencies upon arrival New immigrant information kiosk include: information/brochures and welcome packages in English and French giving personalized information presenting multi-lingual brochures distributing pamphlets on newcomer resources throughout Canada
