Settlement services for newcomer clients living in northern York Region and Simcoe County. Services available: information and resources services to assist in integration (economically and socially) volunteer led English Conversation Circles, where participants practice English skills and meet other newcomers in local community settlement services employment services accreditation and qualification information assistance English language skills other supports to connect newcomers with local services Serves clients by individual appointments at various locations and can deliver to other areas in Simcoe County and York Region throughout the week based on request. English language assessment done in partnership with CLARS Language Assessment Centre.
