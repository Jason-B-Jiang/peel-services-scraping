A program that provides an efficient and humanitarian approach to address the most pressing issues of refugee claimants and other categories of migrants First Contact aims to: Provide accurate information about emergency shelter, social services, legal and health services Walk through orientation and general information that includes a high-level review of the refugee determination process, Interim Federal Health, legal aid, the shelter system, social services, English classes, work permits and more If needed, provide a light meal, tokens or other supports Provide 24/7 telephone information and referral services, focused primarily on after-office-hours referrals for newly arriving refugee claimants
