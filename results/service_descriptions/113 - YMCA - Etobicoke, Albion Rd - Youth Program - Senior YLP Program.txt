NYLD is designed to help newcomer youth have a positive settlement experience and become engaged members of the community. The program offers a safe, supportive, and fun environment where person can meet new people, build new skills and get involved in new home. This program is offered at 8 locations across the GTA, 2 in Home and Community Care Support Services Central West area: Union St. YMCA Centre in Brampton and Albion Rd YMCA Centre at Etobicoke
