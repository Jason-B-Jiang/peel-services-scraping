LINC (Language Instruction for Newcomers to Canada) is a program that provides language instruction in one of Canada's official languages The program is open to all adult immigrants, 18 years of age and over, who are Permanent Residents, Landed Immigrants or Convention Refugees An assessment interview is required to confirm the client is eligible for LINC and their current language proficiency Free childcare offered to participants, for children 18 months and over Free transportation assistance available to eligible participants
