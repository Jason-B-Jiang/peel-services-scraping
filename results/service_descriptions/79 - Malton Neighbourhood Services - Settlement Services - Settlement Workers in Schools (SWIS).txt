Multi-purpose community agency offering settlement services to newcomer families using local schools as the primary point of contact and provide information on schools and education, where to learn English, finding a job, housing, health care, the law, immigration Settlement workers in school guide newcomer parents and students through the Canadian education process The multi-lingual, multicultural SWIS team also: contacts all newcomers in a particular school to help parents, guardians and students learn about the school and community-based services facilitates communication between school staff and newcomers, often helping with interpretation organizes group sessions on relevant topics such as, access to language training, housing issues, education-related workshops, etc. conducts Newcomer Orientation on sessions about schools in August Schools with MNS Settlement Workers
